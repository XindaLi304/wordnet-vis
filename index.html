How to Use

Search

Type a word in the search bar and click Explore or press Enter to expand its semantic network. You can search multiple times — results accumulate on the canvas.

Expand Nodes

Double-click any word node on the canvas to expand its semantic relations (synonyms, antonyms, hypernyms, hyponyms, meronyms, holonyms).

Pathfinding

Hold Ctrl (Windows) or ⌘ Cmd (Mac) and click two nodes. The system will find and display the shortest path between them via the WordNet hypernym tree. Repeat this action on the same two nodes to uncover alternative paths!

Delete Nodes

Select one or more nodes, then press Delete or Backspace to remove the selected nodes and their child subtrees.

Reset Canvas

Click the ↻ reset button next to the search bar to clear the entire canvas and return to the initial state.

View Details

Single-click a node to view its details in the bottom-right panel: definitions, part of speech, and all semantic relations.
